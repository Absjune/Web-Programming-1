Fun Facts:


  • He won The Nobel Prize in Physics 1921

  • Born: 14 March 1879, Ulm, Germany

  • Died: 18 April 1955, Princeton, NJ, USA

  • Prize motivation: “for his services to Theoretical
    Physics,and especially for his discovery of the law of the photoelectric effect”

  • He was offered the Presidency of the State of Israel, which he declined

  • Albert Einstein received honorary doctorate degrees in science, medicine
    and philosophy from many European and American universities

  • During the 1920’s he lectured in Europe, America and the Far East, and he
    was awarded Fellowships or Memberships of all the leading
    scientific academies throughout the world.

  • He married Mileva Maric in 1903 and they had a daughter and two sons

  • Einstein’s gifts inevitably resulted in his dwelling much in
    intellectual solitude and, for relaxation, music played an important part in his life

  • He got divorced in 1919 and the same year got married to his cousin
