Theories That Changed The World:

Quantum Theory of Light

  • all light is composed of tiny packets of energy called photons

Theory of Relativity

  • it is possible for two events to happen simultaneously from the
    perspective of one observer, but occur at different times from the
    perspective of the other

Brownian Motion

  • the existence of atoms ad molecules

The Bose-Einstein Condensate

  • a group of atoms that are cooled very close to absolute zero, and
    behave as if they were a single atom

General Theory of Relativity

  • how the large-scale structure of the universe is set up

The Photoelectric Effect

  • emissions of electrons from metal when light shines on it, as we
    alluded to before

Wave-Particle Duality

  • photons can behave as particles and as waves at the same time
